## Why MoE is important

Modern frontier models increasingly rely on sparse activation:

* **Mixtral 8x7B** → MoE
* **Mixtral 8x22B** → MoE
* **DeepSeek V2/V3** → MoE
* **Qwen3-MoE** → MoE
* Many large internal Google and OpenAI research systems use MoE concepts, even if implementation details aren't fully public.

MoE lets a model have **hundreds of billions of parameters** while activating only a small subset of experts per token, reducing inference cost compared with dense models of similar capacity.
